How It Works

  1. Tag a user on Twitter and use the tip command (e.g., @user @stxtipbot tip 10 STX)
  2. Our bot will reply asking for your wallet address
  3. Reply to the bot with your wallet address
  4. The bot will process the transfer on the Stacks blockchain
  5. Once confirmed, both tipper and recipient will receive a confirmation tweet
